About Me

I am a first year graduate student in the Department of Astronomy at The Ohio State University. I am working with Prof. Scott Gaudi on microlensing searches for exoplanets and the microlensing survey that will be conducted by the Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope.

I was born and raised in Bangalore, India and I finished my schooling from there. I received a BS and MS degree in physics from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Pune in 2023. During my masters thesis, I worked with Dr Anupreeta More and Prof Somak Raychaudhury on strong gravitational lensing of quasars. With Dr Anupreeta More, I developed a pipeline that can conduct an automated search for quadruply lensed quasars in data from large scale surveys.
